Welcome to **Serj.md**.

I've been meaning to start a blog for a while. This is it.

This is where I'll write about things I'm learning, building, and thinking about — mostly software and engineering, but no hard rules.

## Why a blog?

There's something about writing that forces clarity of thought. You think you understand something until you try to explain it. Writing is thinking out loud, and I want to do more of that.

> "If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough."

## What to expect

- **Technical writing** — tools, languages, patterns, and ideas I find interesting
- **Project updates** — what I'm building and what I've learned from it
- **Short notes** — quick thoughts and observations that don't fit neatly anywhere else

No strict schedule. Just writing when I have something worth saying.

Stay tuned.
